Rei's Chicken Tempura


Description

This is a tempura-style fried chicken. Chicken breast or thigh is cut into strips, marinated in soy sauce, garlic, and ginger mixture, coated in tempura batter, and deep-fried.
But what makes tempura different from other fried fare is its distinctive batter. It uses no bread crumbs and less grease than other frying methods.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Plain Flour
  • Corn Flour
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Water
  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori Sheet
  • Avocado
  • White and Black Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Preparation of chicken: Take chicken breast and cut it into thin slices. Do not forget to use a sharp knife. Mix plain flour, corn flour, salt, pepper, and water and give it a good mix. Now add the chicken slices to the chicken tempura batter mix.

  2. Frying the chicken: Take a pan and add oil, put it on heat, and turn the flame on. Let the oil hot on medium heat. Dip the chicken slices into batter then fry it on low-medium flame until golden and crispy. Now remove the chicken from the pan and place it on a tissue to drain excess oil.

  3. Assembly of chicken tempura roll: Now take a nori sheet and place it on a sushi mat. Now spread sushi rice on a nori sheet evenly with a wet hand then add chicken tempura slices and avocado slices.

  4. Rolling the sushi: Roll the nori sheet with your hand with the help of a sushi mat. Use a rolling sushi mat to roll your chicken tempura roll. Donâ€™t forget to roll it with tight hands.

  5. Cutting and serving: Now cut your roll into pieces with a sharp knife and serve it on a serving plate then sprinkle black and white sesame seeds

  6. Enjoy: Your chicken tempura roll is ready to serve. Serve with your desired sauces. Enjoy!
