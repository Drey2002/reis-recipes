Rei's Spicy Ramen Noodles


Description

A Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg.

Rei's Spicy Ramen has a delicious “chew,” or bounciness when eaten that is not found in Italian pastas, soba or even udon.
The noodles are chewy in a way pasta cooked al dente can only dream about.

    • 1 tbsp red miso
    • 1 tbsp white miso

    • Large pot

Steps

  1. Chashu
    2. Step 1: Roll your pork belly into a roll

Step 2: Put in a large pot with all the ingredients

- ³⁄₁₆ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup mirin
- 3 green onion
- ½ cup sake
- ¼ cup ginger
- 4 cloves garlic
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 1 water bottle
- Large pot 

Step 3: Cook for 2-3 hours in low until fully cooked
  2. Broth
    3. Step 1:Put all your bones into a pot and cover them with water 

- 3 lb pork bones
- 1 lb chicken bones

Step 2:Bring them to a boil, once boiled dump out all the water, and refill it back with clean water

Step 3:Simmer on medium low heat for 6hrs, but if you have a pressure cooker it can be done in 4hrs

Step 4:After 6 hours, add 1 white onion, 4 green onions, and ginger and cook for another 2 hrs
  3. Dashi
    4. Step 1:Start by soaking your dried anchovies in water over night

Step 2:Put your soaked anchovies into a pot with your konbu, and shitake mushroom with water to soak everything

Step 3:Bring all that to a simmer but don’t let it boil. Once water is steamy, add your bonito flakes and cook for another 5 minutes

Step 4:Strain your soup and reserve for later, you will be mixing this with the broth
  4. Miso Tare
    5. Step 1:Mix all your misos into a bowl, and toss in all your ingredients except the msg

- 1 tbsp red miso
- 1 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp dashi miso
- 1 cup soy sauce
- ½ cup sake
- ¼ cup mirin
- 2 tsp togarashi
- 4 minced garlics

Step 2:Put on medium low heat and cook until the liquid evaporates

Step 3:Once liquid evaporated, add your msg and cook for another minute

Step 4:Just set to the side once done
  5. Assembly of dish
    6. Step 1:Start off by cooking your chashu and set in the fridge over night

Step 2:Prepare your dashi by soaking your anchovies only. On the next day make the rest of your dashi

Step 3:Make your broth and strain it and leave on heat while you finish everything else

Step 4:Make your tare and once this is done, start assembly

Step 5:Put a tbsp of your tare into a bowl, with your aroma oil and pour some broth into it. Go in with your cooked noodles, your sliced chashu, green onion, soft boil egg, and your narutomaki. Enjoy
